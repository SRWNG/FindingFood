The best burger sauce

Burger sauce is inherently simple. Four ingredients, yet a burst of flavour that instantly levels up your burger game and will make people go, "they know how to cook". Its that simple. (Quick tip: use light mayo over normal mayo to save those calories for the actual burger)

PREP TIME     COOK TIME     SERVES

5 Minutes         N/A                    5-10 Burgers

Ingredients

2 Tbsp Ketchup

3 Tbsp Light Mayonnaise 

1 Tbsp American or Dijon Style Mustard

1/2 Tsp Lemon Juice

 

Method

STEP 1 

Mix all ingredients in a bowl, until smooth and emulsified.
