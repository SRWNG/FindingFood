Tomato Pizza/Pasta Sauce

It hurts me when I see someone using store bought Pizza sauce. Like, its really simple and quick to make, yet it just tastes so much better. Just make this, and you'll agree. I promise

 

PREP TIMECOOK TIMESERVES
N/A10-20 Minutes5-10 Portions

 

Ingredients

1 Can Tomato Pasata or Chopped tomatoes

1 Tbsp Oregano

Pinch Sugar

2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

1 Garlic Clove

 

Method

STEP 1

Heat a pan and add the Olive Oil. Mince the garlic into fine peices and add to pan, stirring until aromatic and light brown.

STEP 2

Once the Garlic is browned, add the Pasata or Chopped Tomatoes and Oregano, stirring for around 5 minutes.

STEP 3

Add the Sugar and Stir for another 5 minutes.

STEP 4

Cut the heat and allow to cool before topping pizza, or mix over pasta until covered evenly.
