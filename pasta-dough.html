Pasta Dough

Pasta Dough can be quite difficult, even though there are only 4 ingredients. It's just about the rolling, keeping it a beautiful texture without making it stick to surfaces. This recipe will definitely make people think you know how to navigate a kitchen. I've also made this recipe infinitely scalable, just for ease. Thank me later.

PREP TIMECOOK TIMESERVES
15 + Resting time1-2 Minutes1 Serving

 

Ingredients

100g 00 Pasta Flour

1 Large Egg

1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Pinch Salt

 

Method

STEP 1

Mix the flour and salt until combined. Pour onto a clean surface and form a well.

STEP 2

Pour the egg and Oil into the middle of the well, and beat until combined, slowly adding flour until it forms a smooth and combined dough.

STEP 3

Sit in a bowl and rest for atleast half an hour. 

STEP 4

Using a pasta maching or bare elbow strength, roll out the dough and cut into strips.

STEP 5

Fill a pan with very salty water and bring to the boil. Dunk the pasta in and cook for about 1-2 minutes or until light and floating.
